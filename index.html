Why did we build this? Why a soda machine?

"This isn't just a marketing piece. We are designing the software for point of sale. It will enable any new device to easily adapt the github packages." This is the first iteration of what can be done when you leverage the technology Dash has baked into its amazing protocol.

Digital Cash

Dash is an open sourced, privacy-centric digital currency with instant transactions. It allows you to keep your finances private as you make transactions without waiting, similar to cash.

Decentralized Governance

This machine was funded via the Masternode Network, which was used to vote on funding this project. All in all the machine was around $2,000 USD and completely funded via the 45/45/10 block reward split.

Instant Transactions via InstantX

Payments are received and confirmed instantly by the other party thanks to InstantX. This soda machine is a prime example of what Dash can provide that other crypto currencies cannot.

Why should you choose Dash?

What makes Dash special, besides being a decentralized digital currency? Dash has several fundamental advantages over other digital currencies. Check out the "What is Dash?" video that summarizes some advantages of using Dash. You can also read the full Dash whitepaper here.

Private

Keep your payments private through a mixing protocol called Darksend.

Instant

Payments are received instantly by the other party thanks to InstantX.

Secure

Advanced encryption and a 2-tier network for complete security.

Low Fees

Much lower than banks or credit cards, often even free. These small fees all go to the network participants. More than 90% of users will be able to fully utilize the Dash network for free...

Peer-to-Peer

Uses a decentralized network, so there is no centralized third party to trust or compromise the network.

Global

You can send money anywhere in the world. Anyone can use Dash and download a Dash Wallet on their smart phone, tablet, or computer.
